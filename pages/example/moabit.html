OSM SVG Extracts

The following SVGs are data exports from OpenStreetMap, compiled through Maperitive. The level of detail depends on the zoom level. Higher Zoom-level more details. The SVGs are optimized for Adobe Illustrator. The SVGs for the higher zoom levels contain a lot of elements, editing in Illustrator and other Vector editing Applications might be tricky. Consider inspecting the layers and deleting all data that is not required. (Advice: Illustrator will probably not allow you to move or scale the SVG with the mouse-tools. You need to use the parameter boxes for width/height,x,y to move and the scale the data.)
