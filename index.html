Refined Berlin

This is a prerelease of Berlin Refined, expect bugs...

The idea of Berlin Refined is to turn OpenData into AccessibleData by providing more acccible ways of finding, downloading and processing OpenData from the Berlin area.

Beyond data, this site tries to explain some basic concepts of the Berlin OpenData infrastructure and how the primary statistical resources are structured and organized. See for example LORs
